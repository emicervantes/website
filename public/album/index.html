Meet my friends…

This page is dedicated to all of my family and my friends’ furry friends!

Midori

This cute little tuxedo cat is Midori. He is a full-time financial analyst at KittyCoin Financial and part-time boxer 💰 This little feline isn’t just crunching numbers – he’s also busy engaging in friendly boxing matches with his brother, Azuki. Midori’s paws might be tiny, but his energy and enthusiasm are boundless as he playfully tussles with Azuki in epic kitty showdowns 🐾 And even though he’s a tough contender in the ring, Midori still relies on the comforting presence of his mom at bedtime, proving that even the bravest of boxers need their snuggles 💕 With a love for both finance and family, Midori is the ultimate adorable package!

Azuki

Meet Azuki, the dynamic border collie bursting with personality! With boundless energy, he’s always on the move, chasing and playing with his big brother Midori. But Azuki’s dreams reach even higher – he aspires to be a professional geese chaser 🐦 and dreams of competing as a track and field Olympian 🏃 And if you think that’s impressive, here’s the secret scoop 🔍 Azuki is also a secret agent, a member of the elite Secret Paw Patrol society, keeping the world safe for all species 🌎

Luka

Meet Luka, a spirited city girl who immerses herself in PhD research at the University of Washington in Seattle. With a focus on the velocity of balls, her thesis captivates her curious mind 🎾 Beyond academia, she dives into her passions, professionally competing in swimming and showcasing her finesse in frisbee catching during her cherished free moments.

Chibi

Chibi, the charming tabby lady cat, was small in size but BIG in personality 💅 She studied Computer Science at Carnegie Meowie University, and she worked as a Software Engineer at Chewey for many years. Her favorite memory was sharing scrumptious bowls of Udon and Soba with Kai🍲

Kai

Kai was a wise old pupper who cherished his naps and savored every bite of brussels sprouts. He pursued Philosophy at Barks University, exploring the profound query ‘What Makes a Good Boy?’📚 🦴
